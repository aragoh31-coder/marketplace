Security Questions

To enable 2FA:

  1. Go to your Account Settings
  2. Click on "Security" tab
  3. Select "Enable 2FA"
  4. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
  5. Enter the verification code to confirm
Tip: We recommend using apps like Aegis or andOTP for maximum security.

PGP (Pretty Good Privacy) provides end-to-end encryption for messages. Benefits include:

  • ‚úÖ Encrypted communication with vendors
  • ‚úÖ Secure 2FA alternative
  • ‚úÖ Identity verification
  • ‚úÖ Protection against phishing

You can add your PGP key in Account Settings ‚Üí PGP Settings.

Always verify you're on the official .onion address:

  1. Bookmark the correct address
  2. Check for the secure padlock icon
  3. Verify the PGP-signed canary
  4. Never access through search engines
Warning: Phishing sites are common. Always double-check the URL.
