Command Line (GPG)

  1. Save the encrypted challenge to a file: 
    cat > challenge.asc << 'EOF'
[Paste encrypted challenge here]
EOF
  2. Decrypt the challenge: 
    gpg --decrypt challenge.asc > challenge.txt
  3. Sign the decrypted message: 
    gpg --clearsign challenge.txt
  4. Copy the contents of challenge.txt.asc and paste in the form above

Troubleshooting
